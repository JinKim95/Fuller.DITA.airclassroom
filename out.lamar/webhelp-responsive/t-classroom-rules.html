Classroom Usage Rules

We have high standards for our classrooms. They are filled with innovative technology, and are spaces that we hope to be kept in pristine condition. We hope that you will see these rules as constructive and helpful in your time in the classroom.

  1. Please do not bring food or drink into the classroom
    I know gamer fuel is often necessary for effective gaming, but please consume your monster and five hour energy before entering the classroom.
  2. Please handle the computers with care both physically and internally
  3. Be sure to keep noise levels down while other classes are in session within the building
    Gaming events can get rowdy, and some noise is absolutely fine, but if things get too loud it may disturb other classrooms and their activities. We recommend making your reservation for after 5 o clock, or even the weekend if possible.
